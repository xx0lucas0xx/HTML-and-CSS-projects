The basics of crystals

Crystals are solid materials that have atoms that form a specific structure and shape. The objects surfaces should also reflect its internal structure.

How do crystals form?

Crystals form when molecules come together and stabilize when certain liquids like magma or when mineral rich water evaporates or from consistent pressure.

How do crystals grow?

Crystals tend to start out small and grow the more atoms are added to the structure through mineral rich water, or even water vapor!
